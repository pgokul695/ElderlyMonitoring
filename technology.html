System Architecture

Wearable Unit Architecture

The portable monitoring device consists of an ESP32 microcontroller integrated with MAX30102 for heart rate and SpO2 monitoring, DS18B20 for temperature sensing, and SIM800L for GSM communication backup. The unit is powered by a 3.7V Li-ion battery with TP4056 charging circuit, ensuring 12-16 hours of continuous operation.

Home Monitoring Unit

The stationary monitoring system utilizes Raspberry Pi 4 as the central processing unit, connected to ESP32-CAM modules for video capture. OpenCV and MediaPipe frameworks enable real-time face detection, emotion recognition, and fall detection through advanced computer vision algorithms.

Cloud Integration Layer

Data transmission occurs through Wi-Fi as the primary channel, with GSM/GPRS as backup. Firebase serves as the real-time database, while ThingSpeak handles IoT analytics. IFTTT integration enables automated workflows and alert generation through multiple channels including SMS, email, and push notifications.
